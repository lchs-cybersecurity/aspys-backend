Email Addresses

You can match email addresses by listing them directly:

mlisano21@mylcusd.net
jlewsadder@lcusd.net

Domains

You can match domains by inserting the .* combination of regex operators to "match anything" from a certain domain:

.*@mylcusd.net
.*@lcusd.net

Including Subdomains

You can match all subdomains of a domain by inserting .* before the domain name:

.*@.*google.com
.*@.*github.com
